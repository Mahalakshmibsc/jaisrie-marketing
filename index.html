Government Subsidy Schemes

Under government schemes, customers can avail central financial assistance for rooftop solar installations such as:

  • Rs.30,000/- for 1 kW systems
  • Rs.60,000/- for 2 kW systems
  • Rs.78,000/- for 3–10 kW systems
